    How it Works

    1

    Start the App

    Launch Reachy Dance Duo from the dashboard. No API keys, no cloud inference, no heavy processing—everything runs locally on the robot.

    2

    Pick a Mode

    Choose Live Groove for real-time analysis of ambient music with targeted beat detection, or Beat Bandit where Reachy sails the high seas of YouTube to procure your content.

    3

    Play Your Music

    In Live Groove, tempo and beats are measured live as music plays. In Beat Bandit, the entire song is analyzed and a full dance sequence is choreographed before playback begins.

    4

    Dance!

    Sit back and watch Reachy Mini move. The choreography plays in sync with the audio for smooth, expressive movements.
